What is Death Literacy?

Death literacy is the practical know-how needed to plan for, care for, and respond to dying, death, and bereavement. It includes legal understanding, emotional preparedness, community support, and the grace to have open conversations about these issues.

Our platform provides research-backed quizzes, personalized feedback, and curated resources to improve your literacy in all areas of end-of-life planning and care.

Inclusive by Design

We are committed to making death literacy accessible across age groups, cultural backgrounds, and communities. Our tools are available in multiple formats, and the platform is designed with empathy and ease of use in mind.
